Maybach 57 S

May 23rd, 2011 by user

Dynamic elegance for the ambitious owner-driver

Self-assured presence and improved technology come perfectly together in the Maybach 57 S. Visual measures, like for example dark tinted glass, headlamp surrounds painted in the vehicle colour, radiator grille with twin louvers, as well as special paint finishes, accentuate its athletic physique and underline its dynamic character.

The amazing performance potential of the Maybach 57 S can be clearly inferred from the lowered ride height of the body and stylish wheel design, which can be treated with the optional ”trowalisation“
ﬁne ﬁnish to give your lightweight-alloy wheels an impressive,
stunning look.

If viewed from the rear, the visible tailpipes of the Maybach 57 S further enhance the dynamic impression of the vehicle.
Since the 2010 facelift, the design has been enhanced through
dark red rear lights and the addition of chrome trim on the boot lid.
More than an automobile, it is an automotive icon which successfully
merges exciting performance and aesthetics with the very best
in sophisticated technologies.

Use your personal touch.
The possible combinations of paint finishes, sports wheel designs and
tinted glass are formidable and you can be confident of creating
the ideal result, thus showing the world your own style.

The road stretches ahead of you. There‘s still a long way to go.
It‘s at times like this that you appreciate the effortless control you have over the impressive engine of your Maybach 57 S to the full: the most powerful engine available in our range is dominated by the advanced brake system that ensures stable and comfortable handling in every driving situation.

With its clean lines, the perfectly harmonized interior of
the Maybach 57 S has a unique and unmistakably dynamic appearance. The two-tones leather combination in connection with
high-gloss trim elements in piano lacquer are contrasted details
which make this model very valued by connoisseurs.

Nothing is left to chance in this superb vehicle. For example, seat pipings, optionally even hand-woven, propose a perfect contrast
to the carbon-fibre trim elements. All those features ensure your senses a particularly comfortable interior ambience.

In addition to featuring luxury appointments and dynamic design,
the Maybach 57 S is characterised by outstanding engineering and effortlessly superior performance – an exceptional combination
of strengths, even in the high-end luxury segment.
Reduced fuel consumption and emissions with increased engine output
in the Maybach facelift have been added to the pleasure of the driving
experience.
An exceptional car – created for the ambitious owner-driver.
