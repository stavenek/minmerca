Maybach engineering

October 27th, 2011 by user

All Maybach automobiles have one thing in common: each one is unique.

Every Maybach is a German masterpiece, hand-made by the most experienced and skilled Daimler engineers and specialists. The care for detail that our engineers show guarantees the best results in terms of comfort, luxury, outstanding technology, craftsmanship and individuality.

State-of-the-art technology and an extensive range of comfort-oriented features confirm the position of the Maybach brand at the forefront of the high-end luxury segment and ensure a driving experience of the highest quality.
