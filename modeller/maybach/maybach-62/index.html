Maybach 62

May 23rd, 2011 by user

Luxury that makes you feel at home even on the move

With its classic, timeless style and the exceptional possibilities of individualisation, the Maybach 62 embodies all the values on which
the legendary reputation of the Maybach marque is based.

Its distinctive and self-assured design, further enhanced by its impressive dimensions – 6.17 meters in length – put the vehicle more than ever in an entirely new dimension in terms of luxury and comfort in the chauffeur-driven class, categorically reflecting the Maybach marque’s leading position in this sector. Majestically sculpted contours underline the distinguished presence of this model which embodies a contemporary interpretation of status, comfort and prestige.

The optional two tone paint schemes and three different wheel designs underline the vehicle’s remarkably elegant character in an accomplished synthesis of style and substance.
Above all, the generous dimensions of this luxurious vehicle allow to stand out of the crowd, showing a dazzling appearance.

The unique rear passenger compartment is just one of the many compelling reasons to choose a Maybach 62. A vast array of comfort and convenience features, as well as luxury details, reflects an exceptional sense of style and class to be enjoyed in complete privacy.

The exceptional spaciousness offered by the rear passenger compartment make it the perfect setting for those who always want to travel at the uppermost level of class and comfort. In order to provide an exceptionally comfortable environment, Maybach has exclusively created unique reclining rear seats with integrated legrests and footrests. The seats are upholstered using only the finest leathers that have been skilfully combined with trim elements bestowing additional grace on your personal space.

The designers and interior appointments specialists at the Maybach Manufaktur are constantly seeking to make the interior of every Maybach even more distinctive and exclusive. Their attention on the Maybach 62 was focused in particular on the interior touches which are unique even in this class of vehicles: for instance an optional electrotransparent partition, that can be embellished with a motif of your choice, ensures complete separation between the chauffeur and the passenger compartment, warranting you absolute privacy, maybe to enjoy a movie of your choice on the optional 19” cinema screen.

In addition to the opulent spaciousness, featuring luxury appointments and timeless design, the Maybach 62 is characterised by outstanding engineering and effortlessly premium performance – an exceptional combination of strengths, even in the high-end luxury segment. Reduced fuel consumption and emissions in the Maybach facelift have been added to the pleasure of the driving experience. An exceptional car – created for the upper class.
